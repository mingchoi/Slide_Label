Slide Label

How to use:

$("#myLink").Slide_Label( $("<div id='myDiv' style='width: 150px' >content</div>") );
or
$('#myLink').Slide_Label(
  $('<div>', {id: 'myDiv', style: 'width: 150px', text: 'content'})
)

example:

Just move your mouse to here and then the label will appear.

You can style your own css like this by editing .Slide_Label_Gap and the div which you have send into the function.
